About Myself

In research:

My research interests lie in the fields of Deep Learning learning and Natural Language Processing. My PhD research was about improving Neural Machine Translation (NMT) in bilingually scarce scenarios. Like most deep learning approaches, NMT is notorious for its dependence on large amounts of training data. My research was focused on leveraging curated monolingual resources for compensating this requirement.
